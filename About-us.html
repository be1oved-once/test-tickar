About Beforexam

Beforexam is an online CA Foundation practice platform designed to help students revise smarter through MCQs, RTP/MTP-based tests, chapter-wise practice, and instant evaluation.

Built by a CA Foundation student, Beforexam focuses on one simple idea: practice before the exam, not just study. The platform is optimized for mobile-first learners in India who want quick, distraction-free revision.

Beforexam is not a coaching institute or textbook replacement. It works as a daily practice companion that keeps students connected with their syllabus consistently.

What Beforexam Helps You Do

  • Practice CA Foundation MCQs chapter-wise
  • Revise using RTP and MTP pattern questions
  • Test yourself in timed exam-like conditions
  • Get instant evaluation and correction
  • Track performance, accuracy, and consistency

The platform is especially useful for students who want to revise in short time slots instead of long study sessions.

हमारा उद्देश्य (Purpose)

आज के समय में ज़्यादातर छात्र मोबाइल का उपयोग करते हैं, लेकिन मोबाइल पढ़ाई से ज़्यादा distraction बन चुका है। Beforexam इसी gap को fill करता है।

हमारा उद्देश्य है मोबाइल को distraction से productivity में बदलना, ताकि छात्र रोज़ थोड़ा-थोड़ा practice कर सकें - बिना pressure के।

Who Built Beforexam

My name is Kanhaiya Chaudhary. I am an 18-year-old CA Foundation student. Beforexam was created from personal experience of struggling with consistency, revision gaps, and exam pressure.

This platform was first built as a personal revision tool and later expanded for other CA Foundation students facing the same challenges.

Every feature is designed from a student’s perspective - focusing on speed, clarity, and usefulness instead of complexity.

Why Beforexam Is Different

  • No unnecessary features or distractions
  • Focused only on CA Foundation syllabus
  • Mobile-friendly and fast loading
  • Practice-oriented, not content-heavy
  • Designed for consistency, not cramming

Mission

The mission of Beforexam is to help CA Foundation students build a daily practice habit using a simple, reliable, and accessible platform.

  • Encourage regular MCQ practice
  • Reduce last-minute exam pressure
  • Make revision measurable and honest
  • Support exam-oriented preparation

Vision

Beforexam aims to grow into a trusted online practice environment where students can revise, test, analyze, and improve continuously.

In the future, Beforexam may follow a freemium model where:

  • Core practice features remain free
  • Advanced analytics may be optional
  • Accessibility is never compromised

Message for CA Foundation Students

Consistent study is difficult, especially when motivation is low. Beforexam is built for those days when you want to study, but don’t know where to start.

Even solving a few MCQs daily builds confidence. Small efforts, repeated daily, create strong exam readiness.

“You don’t need perfect study days. You need consistent practice.”
