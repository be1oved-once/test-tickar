About TIC.Kar

TIC.Kar is a student-focused academic practice platform created to make revision simple, fast, and effective - especially for learners who rely heavily on mobile devices for study.

The platform is built with the belief that consistent practice matters more than long study hours. TIC.Kar is not designed to replace textbooks, coaching institutes, or classrooms, but to act as a reliable daily companion for students preparing for competitive and professional examinations.

TIC.Kar focuses on MCQ-based practice, chapter-wise testing, RTP/MTP-style preparation, performance tracking, and quick revision, helping students stay connected with their syllabus every day.

हमारा उद्देश्य है मोबाइल को distraction से productivity में बदलना, ताकि छात्र छोटे-छोटे समय में भी meaningful practice कर सकें।

Who Built This Platform

My name is Kunal Sharma. I am 18 years old and currently a CA Foundation student.

I am not a professional developer. I am a regular student who understands the real challenges of daily revision, motivation drops, and inconsistent study habits. TIC.Kar was developed as a personal solution first - and later expanded for other students facing the same problems.

This platform is built from a student’s perspective, keeping simplicity, speed, and practicality as top priorities.

Mission

Our mission is to help students develop a habit of daily practice through a clean, fast, and accessible platform that works equally well on mobile and desktop.

  • Encourage consistency over cramming
  • Make revision simple and measurable
  • Support exam-oriented preparation
  • Reduce dependency on heavy study setups

Vision

The long-term vision of TIC.Kar is to grow into a trusted academic practice environment where students can revise, test, analyze, and improve - without feeling overwhelmed.

In the future, TIC.Kar may adopt a freemium model:

  • Core practice features will remain free for all students
  • Advanced analytics, structured tests, and premium tools may be optional
  • No compromise on accessibility or fairness

Message for Students

Studying consistently is difficult - especially when motivation is low. TIC.Kar is built for those days when you don’t feel like studying, but still want to do something meaningful.

Even solving a few questions daily is progress. Small efforts, repeated daily, lead to big results. Stay consistent. Stay honest with your preparation.

“You don’t need perfect days. You need consistent ones.”

Invitation to Teachers & Educators

TIC.Kar also serves as a controlled test environment for educators. Teachers are welcome to collaborate for:

  • Creating and testing MCQ-based assessments
  • Experimenting with restricted or timed tests
  • Analyzing student performance trends
  • Designing chapter-wise or revision-focused tests

The platform supports features like limited attempts, timers, performance summaries, and review control, making it suitable for experimental academic use.

Collaboration Opportunities

TIC.Kar is open to collaboration with:

  • Teachers who want a digital practice environment
  • Students who wish to contribute quality questions
  • Individuals with ideas to improve learning workflows

This is a learning-first initiative, continuously improved through feedback and real student usage.
